Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account?

Simply click on "Join Now" and fill out the registration form. It takes less than 2 minutes to get started.

Is Télétravail free to use?

Yes! Creating an account and browsing jobs is completely free for professionals. We only charge companies for posting positions.

How long does it take to hear back from companies?

Response times vary by company, but most employers review applications within 5-7 business days.

Can I apply to multiple positions?

Absolutely! You can apply to as many positions as you like. We recommend tailoring your application for each role.
